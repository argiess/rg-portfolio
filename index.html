Frequently Asked Questions

A static website is a site that consists of fixed content – the same information is displayed to every visitor. It doesn't rely on databases or dynamic updates, making it faster, more secure, and easier to maintain.

Static websites are perfect for small businesses that don’t require constant updates or advanced functionalities like e-commerce. They load quickly, are highly secure, and are more affordable to develop and host than dynamic websites.

The cost varies depending on your specific needs, but static websites tend to be more affordable than dynamic sites due to their simplicity. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your business.

While static websites are not designed for frequent updates, we can implement simple tools or guides for you to make basic content changes, such as updating text or images. Alternatively, we offer affordable update services.

Absolutely! We build all our websites with SEO best practices, helping your site rank higher on search engines like Google, so more customers can find you.

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project, but most static websites are ready within 1-2 weeks from the start of the project.

We’ll need key information about your business, such as services offered, contact details, and any images or branding materials (like a logo) you’d like to include. Don’t worry, we’ll guide you through the process!

Yes! Static websites are naturally more secure than dynamic sites because they don't rely on databases, reducing the risk of hacking or malware. We also recommend using HTTPS for added security.

No ongoing maintenance is required for static websites. Since there’s no complex backend system, they are stable and require fewer updates, saving you time and money.
